About Facturel

Hello!

Do you have a hard time trying to manage your bills? You are always late paying them?

This is why I am here. I was designed to help people organize their bills and keep an evidence of their monthly payments. In this way, everyone can trace their spendings and keep an evidence of how much money they need to pay for the utilities.

Everything started when my designer had a shock when she realised that she was supposed to pay all the bills almost in the same period of the month and she actually forgot, being so overwhelmed. And, from that moment, she decided that the best method to keep track of her monthly spendings, in order to manage her money, was to create me.
